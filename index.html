Malibu Nights by LANY

The audio track "Malibu Nights" is a song performed by the music group LANY. It serves as the title track of their second studio album, released in 2018 under the same name, "Malibu Nights". The song was written by LANY band members Paul Klein, Jake Goss, and Les Priest.

The "Malibu Nights" music video captures a sense of nostalgia, longing, and melancholy, featuring scenes that encompass personal memories, travels, and intimate moments between the band members. The video effectively conveys the same message as the song itself, presenting a strong and profound depiction of the emotions experienced after the end of a relationship.

The lyrics of "Malibu Nights" depict the nighttime scenery of Malibu as the backdrop for a story of lost love. The song expresses longing and emptiness experienced after losing someone deeply loved, while attempting to let go of lingering memories.
